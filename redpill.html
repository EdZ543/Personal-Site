Introducing RedPill

Posted by Albert Lai on November 14, 2019

Our mission at RedPill to skyrocket human productivity by allowing humans to download skills into the mind to exponentially increase the speed of learning. I’m working with Shifra Khan and Peter Ma to advance the field and bring our ultimate plan to fruition!



The Technology

Our plan is to use multiple methods of stimuli to manipulate the mind to learn complex tasks. Currently, we’ve made tremendous progress in the fields of neurology and brain computer interfaces, many of which can be combined together to eventually create downloadable skills.

We've broken down the core components into three main categories: Brain Stimulation, State and Memory. Brain stimulation help allow the patient to mimic desired tasks. Then, the state of the mind is altered to allow retention of information. Finally, we commit the learnt skills into long term memory. Simple.
